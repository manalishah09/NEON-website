Know us...

Neon Mitesh- Accountancy Firm, has acquired, in course of time, a reputation as a single window professional service firm rendering excellent quality services in the areas of Audit & Assurance, taxation, financial and management consultancy, regulatory compliances and business valuations. In its journey, the firm has built up a strong efficient and effective team of partners and other professionals having high expertise in the relevant areas of functioning, enabling the firm to deliver utmost satisfaction to the clients. The firm serves a wide range of clientele across the various industries, including financial sector. Our highly skilled and strongly motivated team enables us to render the best professional services to the clients, exceeding their expectations.
